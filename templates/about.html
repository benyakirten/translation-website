About Me

I am a writer who's trying to get into programming. I'm fully fluent in Italian and can figure my way most romance languages. I am a member of Phi Beta Kappa and have degrees in Linguistics, Classics and summa cum laude in Italian. I lived in Italy for two years before moving back to Colorado.

About This Website

This page is an implementation of my r2api package to translate recipes and their units from Italian to English. Hopefully besides giving me practice with a bunch of uses of CSS and basic web design, people will be able to find the website useful.

Technologies Used

Flask, Sass, r2api, Javascript, a few simple illustrations (mostly image tracing)
